Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Msg4 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application that integrates with Meta's Facebook and Instagram services.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Meta accounts with Msg4, we collect the following information:

  • Facebook Page ID: Your unique Facebook Page identifier
  • Instagram Business Account ID: Your Instagram Business Account identifier linked to your Facebook Page
  • Direct Messages: Messages from your Instagram Business Account used solely for automation purposes
  • Page Access Tokens: Authentication tokens required to manage your automation workflows
  • Automation Configuration Data: Welcome messages and automated reply settings you create

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To enable chatbot automation on your Instagram Business Account
  • To deliver welcome messages and automated responses to your followers
  • To manage and store your automation configurations
  • To provide technical support and troubleshooting
  • To improve our service functionality and user experience
Important: All collected data is used exclusively for automating your Instagram Business Account messaging. We do NOT share your data with third parties for any purposes other than providing the core automation service.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information and Meta data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data and access tokens
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure authorization
  • Restricted access to data on a need-to-know basis
  • Compliance with industry security standards

5. Third-Party Sharing

Your data is NOT shared with third parties. We maintain strict policies against selling, renting, or sharing your personal information and Meta data with any external organizations. Your Instagram Business Account data is used solely by Msg4 to provide the automation service you have authorized.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about your account
  • Request deletion of your data and automation configurations
  • Disconnect your Meta accounts from Msg4 at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request information about how your data is used

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the automation service. When you disconnect your Meta accounts or request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where required by law to maintain records for compliance purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Msg4 is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

9. Meta Data Compliance

Msg4 operates in compliance with Meta's Platform Policies and Data Use Policies. All usage of Facebook Page and Instagram Business Account data is subject to Meta's terms of service. For more information about how Meta handles your data, please review Meta's Privacy Policy at https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy and updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of Msg4 after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
